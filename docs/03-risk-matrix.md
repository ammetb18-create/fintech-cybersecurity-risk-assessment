# Risk Matrix

This matrix evaluates key risks based on likelihood and impact to determine overall risk level and required controls.

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Risk Level | Control |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|--------|
| Identity Fraud | High | High | Critical | Identity verification processes, document validation |
| Data Breach | High | High | Critical | Encryption, access control, monitoring |
| Data Integrity Issues | High | Medium | High | Data validation checks, system controls |
| Phishing Attacks | Medium | High | High | Security awareness training, email filtering |
| Insider Threat | Low | High | Medium | Role-based access control, monitoring |
| Weak Access Control | Medium | High | High | Least privilege access, MFA |

---

## Risk Level Definitions

- **Critical**: Immediate action required
- **High**: High priority mitigation needed
- **Medium**: Monitor and improve controls
