# Recommendations & Conclusion

## Recommendations

Based on the identified risks and analysis, the following actions are recommended:

### Technical Recommendations

- Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) across all critical systems
- Encrypt sensitive customer data at rest and in transit
- Strengthen system monitoring and logging capabilities

---

### Operational Recommendations

- Standardize identity verification processes across all applications
- Improve data validation controls to reduce inconsistencies
- Conduct regular internal audits of data accuracy and access

---

### Legal & Compliance Recommendations

- Strengthen data protection policies and procedures
- Improve documentation of data handling practices
- Align security practices with regulatory expectations for financial services

---

## Conclusion

The digital lending platform faces significant cybersecurity and fraud-related risks, particularly in identity verification, data protection, and data integrity.

By implementing structured cybersecurity controls and integrating legal and compliance awareness, the organization can significantly reduce risk exposure and improve operational resilience.

This project demonstrates how cybersecurity risk analysis supports not only technical protection, but also business, legal, and regulatory stability.
