# Legal & Compliance Considerations

This section outlines key legal and compliance risks associated with handling sensitive customer data in a digital lending platform.

## Data Protection Risk

Digital lending platforms collect personally identifiable information (PII), including identity documents, financial data, contact information, and credit-related records.

Failure to properly protect this data may result in regulatory exposure, legal liability, reputational damage, and loss of customer trust.

---

## Identity Verification & Fraud Risk

Weak identity verification may allow fraudulent applications, incorrect customer records, and financial losses.

Strong document validation and data consistency checks help reduce fraud risk and support reliable decision-making.

---

## Data Integrity & Record Accuracy

Accurate records are essential for risk evaluation, lending decisions, fraud prevention, and compliance.

Data inconsistencies may lead to incorrect approvals, operational errors, and compliance issues.

---

## Key Insight

Cybersecurity is not only a technical issue. It is also a legal, compliance, and business risk responsibility.

Integrating legal risk awareness into cybersecurity assessments strengthens organizational resilience and protects customer data.
