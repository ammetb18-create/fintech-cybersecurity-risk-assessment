# Executive Summary

This cybersecurity risk assessment evaluates the main security, fraud, operational, and compliance risks faced by a simulated digital lending platform.

The company collects and processes sensitive customer information for online loan applications, including identity documents, personal data, financial information, and credit-related records.

The assessment identifies key risks such as identity fraud, data breach, phishing, unauthorized access, data integrity failures, and weak incident response procedures.

The purpose of this project is to demonstrate how cybersecurity risk analysis can support business protection, regulatory awareness, fraud prevention, and customer data security in the fintech sector.

This project also connects cybersecurity risk with legal and compliance considerations, especially when handling personally identifiable information and financial data.
